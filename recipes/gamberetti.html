Con gamberetti e rucola

Pasta con gamberetti is a pasta dish made typically with shrimps, olive oil, garlic, chili, tomatopaste and rucola.

This dish is very easy to make, quickly done and full of taste. A big favorite in Italy.

ingredients for 4 people

  • fresh spaghetti 400gr
  • 4 gloves of garlic, chopped
  • 1 red chili pepper, chopped
  • 400gr of shrimps
  • 200gr of tomatopaste
  • 1 lemon, squeezed
  • 100 gr of rucola
  • 6 tablespoons of extra virgine olive oil
  • salt and pepper

step by step

  1. Cook the spaghetti in boiling water with plenty of added salt for about 10 minutes till al dente. Save a cup of the cooking water
  2. Bake the garlic and chili in a pan with the olive oil on medium heat
  3. Add the shrimps and bake for 2 mins
  4. Add the tomatopaste and let the sauce simmer for 10mins
  5. Mix the spaghetti through the sauce and add the lemon and the rucola
